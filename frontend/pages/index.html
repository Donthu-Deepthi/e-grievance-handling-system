About Our System

A simple digital platform that allows villagers to submit complaints about local issues—such as damaged roads, poor water supply, or sanitation concerns—without needing to visit the Panchayat office physically.

Our Core Goals

  • Enable easy grievance submission with photo uploads
  • Provide real-time complaint tracking
  • Reduce physical visits to Panchayat office
  • Improve transparency and accountability
  • Maintain digital records for reporting

How It Works

Simple four-step process to submit and track your grievances

1

Register & Login

Create your account or login to the system using your mobile number or email

2

Submit Complaint

Select issue category, add description, and upload photos for clarity

3

Track Status

Monitor your complaint status in real-time (Pending, Accepted, Resolved)

4

Get Resolution

Receive updates and confirmation when your issue is resolved

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the grievance system

How do I register a complaint?

To register a complaint:

  1. Click on "Register Complaint" button
  2. Create an account or login
  3. Select the appropriate category
  4. Fill in details and upload photos if available
  5. Submit your complaint
How can I track my complaint status?

After logging in, go to "My Complaints" section where you can see the real-time status of all your submitted grievances. You'll receive notifications for status updates.

Do I need to visit the Panchayat office?

No physical visit is required. The entire process is digital. However, for complex issues, officials may contact you for additional information or site visit.

What types of issues can I report?

You can report various local issues including road damage, water supply problems, sanitation issues, electricity problems, agricultural concerns, and education-related issues.

Is there a mobile app available?

Currently, we have a mobile-responsive website that works well on all devices. A dedicated mobile app is planned for future release.
